He Cares

Quote from 54-0724 A Personal Experience With God

But even the very hairs of your head are all numbered. Fear not therefore: ye are of more value than many sparrows.

Luke 12:7

Throughout the Message and the Bible, we see God is interested in more than just the “highlights” in our lives. He is so concerned with every detail that He knows the very number of hairs on our head. Our God, who created the vast galaxies and solar systems, knows something that seems as insignificant as that!

Just as nothing is too great for our Lord Jesus, nothing is too small for Him either. This family had an annoying problem with ants invading their home. Like Brother Branham prayed for the hornets in his yard, this family prayed for the ants. Here is their testimony.

We live in a very old farmhouse, and to be honest it is in very good condition. But like any old houses, it has cracks and crevices that little creatures find their way into.

Unfortunately, we had been dealing with flying ants. We searched from the housetop to the bottom to find where they were coming from, but just could not find it. We finally decided we would have to get some kind of spray or traps and see if that would work, but we didn't have much time.

In just a few days we were having guests stay with us. We needed a solution and we needed one fast. Then it came on my mind of the testimony that posted on this website not too long ago about the family who needed furniture, and God provided them exactly what they needed. All I needed to do was say a simple sincere prayer, and I knew God would take care of it.

I got on my knees and asked God to relocate the ants somewhere they would prosper, far from us and other people. And praise God, they haven't been a problem since then! I have no doubt He relocated those ants.

I love how He careth for each of us, no matter how big or small the request. Oh, how we love Him!

God bless you.

Sister Kayla
